Frequently asked questions

How does a synthetic audience differ from a traditional survey? expand_more
Our synthetic agents don't just answer multiple-choice questions; they hold persistent beliefs, interact with each other, and simulate belief drift over time based on specific stimuli, providing deeper behavioral insights than static surveys.
What is a "binding deliverable"? expand_more
A binding deliverable means we guarantee the exact outputs listed in your package (e.g., 6h PDF summary, heat-map) within the specified timeframe. No scope creep, no hidden fees.
Can we customize the personas? expand_more
Yes. During onboarding, we calibrate the standard archetypes provided in the package to align closely with your specific target audience or stakeholder matrix.
How is the data secured? expand_more
All simulations run in isolated, localized containers. Your proprietary inputs (draft policies, unreleased ad concepts) are never used to train generalized models outside of your specific instance.
What does "Africa-grounded data" actually mean? expand_more
Most foundational models default to Western cultural contexts. We specifically tune our agent parameters using regional socio-economic data, dialect nuances, and local media consumption patterns for accurate regional simulations.
Do you offer enterprise retainers? expand_more
Yes, for high-frequency needs, we offer custom platform access and continuous monitoring packages. Please contact our sales team to discuss enterprise scaling.
