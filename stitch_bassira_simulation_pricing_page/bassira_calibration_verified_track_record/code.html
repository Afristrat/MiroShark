How we count it

We employ a strict Proper Scoring Rule. Our agents don't just say "yes" or "no"; they provide a probability distribution. If an agent says there's a 70% chance of an event, we track how often those 70% calls actually happen.

Our Brier score (0.18) reflects our commitment to calibration. A score of 0 is perfect prescience; a score of 0.25 is random guessing. We consistently outperform institutional consensus.

Data is updated in real-time as simulations reach their resolution horizon. No forecasts are ever deleted or "restated."
