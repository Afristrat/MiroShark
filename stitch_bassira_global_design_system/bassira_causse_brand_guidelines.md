# BASSIRA CAUSSE MASTER PALETTE & TYPOGRAPHY REFERENCE

## Cultural Calibration
Dual MENA (Morocco, GCC) + European (France, UK) audience.

## Color Palette

### PRIMARY ACTIONS & CONFIDENCE
- **Warm Orange (#FF8551)**
  - **MENA:** Sun, generosity, celebration, decisive energy.
  - **Europe:** Innovation, approachability.
  - **Use:** CTAs, highlights, active states, key numbers.

### TRUST & METHODOLOGICAL AUTHORITY
- **Deep Mint-Green (#006D44)**
  - **MENA:** Trustworthiness, growth, Islam, paradise.
  - **Europe:** Sustainability, reliability, safety.
  - **Use:** Success states, validation badges, trust signals, verified data.

### PREMIUM SURFACE & CLARITY
- **Cream Off-White (#FAF7F2)**
  - **MENA:** Marble, parchment, manuscript (refinement and wisdom).
  - **Europe:** Artisanal luxury, Bauhaus minimalism, editorial quality.
  - **Use:** Primary background, breathing room, hero sections.

### AUTHORITY WITHOUT COLDNESS
- **Warm Charcoal-Brown (#241915)**
  - **MENA:** Earth, roots, ink (tradition + modernity).
  - **Europe:** Serious professionalism, warmer than pure black.
  - **Use:** Primary text, headlines, borders of importance.

### URGENCY WITHOUT AGGRESSION
- **Deep Terracotta (#A13F0F)**
  - **MENA:** Tadelakt, clay, desert (Moroccan craft luxury).
  - **Europe:** Warmth, artisanal quality.
  - **Use:** Primary CTAs, pricing emphasis, featured badges.

## Forbidden Combinations
- ✗ **Red (#FF4444)** as primary (aggression in MENA).
- ✗ **Pure black backgrounds** (funeral associations).
- ✗ **Yellow** as primary (cheapness signal in GCC).
- ✗ **High-saturation blue** as dominant (too "tech startup generic").

## Typography

### OUTFIT (Headings, Display, Numbers)
- **Role:** Authority + geometric friendliness.
- **Weights:** 400, 500, 600, 700 (hero only).
- **Letter-spacing:** H1 -0.02em, H2 -0.01em.
- **Min size:** 14px.

### MANROPE (Body, UI Labels, Descriptions)
- **Role:** High-legibility for dense data.
- **Weights:** 400, 500, 600.
- **Line-height:** 1.6 (body), 1.2 (labels).
- **Max size:** 24px.

### TAJAWAL (Arabic Content)
- **Role:** Premium Arabic type.
- **Formality:** Modern Standard Arabic (fusha) for B2B.
- **Direction:** RTL, logical CSS.

### JETBRAINS MONO (Technical Data)
- **Role:** Technical credibility, precision signals.
- **Use:** IDs, API keys, probability numbers, agent logs.
